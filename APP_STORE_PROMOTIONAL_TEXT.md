## Tips for App Store Submission

1. **Screenshots**: Use real device screenshots, not simulator
2. **App Icon**: Use your purple cross icon (120x120 minimum)
3. **Preview Video**: Consider creating a short video showcasing key features
4. **Localization**: Consider translating to other languages if you plan to expand
5. **Keywords**: Update keywords based on App Store search trends
6. **Reviews**: Encourage satisfied users to leave reviews
