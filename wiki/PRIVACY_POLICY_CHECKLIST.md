## Template Sections

If you need help drafting, here are template sections:

### Section: "Live Streaming Data"

```markdown
## Live Streaming

When you use our live streaming features, we collect and process the following data:

**Audio and Video Data:**
- We access your device's camera and microphone to enable live streaming
- Video and audio streams are transmitted in real-time to other session participants
- Stream data is processed through Agora RTC SDK infrastructure
- We do not permanently record or store video/audio streams
- Streams are transmitted in real-time and not saved to our servers

**Session Data:**
- Chat messages sent during live sessions are stored
- Participant lists and session information are stored
- Invitation codes and session metadata are stored

**Data Sharing:**
- Your video and audio are shared with other participants in your session
- Stream data is routed through Agora's global infrastructure
- We use Agora SDK (see Third-Party Services) for streaming infrastructure

**Your Control:**
- You can control who joins your sessions through invitation codes
- You can leave sessions at any time
- You can block or report users who engage in inappropriate behavior
- You can disable camera/microphone during sessions
```
