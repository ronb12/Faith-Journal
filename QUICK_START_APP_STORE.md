## ⚠️ Important: Privacy Policy URL

You MUST have a privacy policy URL for App Store submission. Options:

1. **Create a simple web page** with your privacy policy (copy from your app's Privacy Policy view)
2. **Use a free service** like https://www.privacypolicies.com
3. **Host it on GitHub Pages** (free)

Once you have the URL, add it in App Store Connect > App Information > Privacy Policy URL
