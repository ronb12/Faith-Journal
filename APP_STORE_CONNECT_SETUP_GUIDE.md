## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Log into App Store Connect
1. Go to https://appstoreconnect.apple.com
2. Sign in with your Apple ID (the one associated with your developer account)
3. You may need to complete 2FA verification

### Step 2: Select Your App
1. Click on **"My Apps"**
2. Find and click on **"Faith Journal"** (or create it if it doesn't exist)

### Step 3: Add Subtitle
1. In the **"App Information"** tab (left sidebar)
2. Scroll to **"Subtitle"** field
3. Paste: `Daily Faith & Prayer Journal`
4. Click **"Save"** (top right)

### Step 4: Add Promotional Text
1. In the **"App Store"** tab (left sidebar)
2. Select your app version (or "1.0 Prepare for Submission" if first time)
3. Scroll down to **"Promotional Text"** section
4. Paste the promotional text:
   ```
   ‚ú® NEW: Enhanced Bible Study with progress tracking, favorites, and interactive questions. Start your faith journey today with daily verses, prayer tracking, and personalized devotionals!
   ```
5. Click **"Save"** (top right)

### Step 5: Add Description
1. Still in the **"App Store"** tab
2. Scroll to **"Description"** field
3. Copy the full description from `APP_STORE_PROMOTIONAL_TEXT.md`
4. Paste it into the Description field
5. Click **"Save"**

### Step 6: Add Keywords
1. Still in the **"App Store"** tab
2. Scroll to **"Keywords"** field
3. Paste (NO SPACES after commas):
   ```
   faith,journal,prayer,Bible,study,devotional,spiritual,Christian,religion,reflection,mood,daily,verse
   ```
4. Click **"Save"**

### Step 7: Add What's New (Release Notes)
1. In the **"Version Information"** section
2. Scroll to **"What's New in This Version"**
3. Copy the release notes from `APP_STORE_PROMOTIONAL_TEXT.md`
4. Paste and click **"Save"**

### Step 8: Add Support URL
1. In **"App Information"** tab
2. Scroll to **"Support URL"**
3. Enter your support email or website:
   - `mailto:support@faithjournal.app` (if email)
   - OR `https://faithjournal.app/support` (if website)

### Step 9: Add Marketing URL (Optional)
1. In **"App Information"** tab
2. Scroll to **"Marketing URL"** (optional)
3. Enter: `https://faithjournal.app` (or leave blank if you don't have a website)

### Step 10: Add Privacy Policy URL
1. In **"App Information"** tab
2. Scroll to **"Privacy Policy URL"** (required for App Store submission)
3. Options:
   - Use in-app privacy policy: `https://faithjournal.app/privacy`
   - OR create a simple web page with your privacy policy
   - OR use a service like https://www.privacypolicies.com
